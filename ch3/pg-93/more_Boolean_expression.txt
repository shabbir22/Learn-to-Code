Take out your pencil and put some more Boolean expressions through their paces.
For each expression below, compute its value and write in your answer.

age>5 and age<10

When age is 6, what does this evaluate to?_ True
When age is 11, what does this evaluate to?_ False
When age is 5, what does this evaluate to?_ False

age>5 or age==3

When age is 6, what does this evaluate to?_ True
When age is 2, what does this evaluate to?_ False
When age is 3, what does this evaluate to?_ True

not (age>5)

When age is 6, what does this evaluate to?_ False
When age is 2, what does this evaluate to?_ True
